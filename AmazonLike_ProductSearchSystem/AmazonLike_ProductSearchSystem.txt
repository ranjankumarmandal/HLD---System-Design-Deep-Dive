Functional Requirement:
------------------------
- Accepts user query (keywords, filters, sort options), returns paginated product results.
- Returns search suggestions as the user types
- Adds a new product to the catalog
- Updates product info (admin / internal)
- Remove a product (admin / internal)
- Fetches details for a product (used for product pages)
- (Optional) recommendations

Non-Functional Requirement:
---------------------------
- Low latency
- High availability
- Massive scale
- (and so on...)

Assumptions:
------------
- 10M users, 1M daily searches, peak 2k searches / sec.
-- Traffic: At peak - internal traffic is higher
-- Heavy Load: Black friday or sales could spike traffic by 10x. Auto-scale search nodes, use caching for trending queries, and maintain a hot index for bestsellers.
-- Slow Times: Scale down workers, cache TTL can be longer
